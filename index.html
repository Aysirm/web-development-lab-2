Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A traditional Italian comfort food loved around the world.

Recipe Overview

This spaghetti bolognese recipe has been passed down through generations. Itâ€™s rich, hearty, and perfect for a cozy family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz ground beef (use 85/15 or 80/20 for best flavor - dont go too lean)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped(yellow or white onion works great)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced(fresh is essential for that aromatic punch)
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes(you can also use fresh tomatoes, just make sure to cook them down)
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1 cup beef broth (low sodium is preferred)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano (optional)
  • 1 tsp dried basil (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste(start with 1/2 tsp each)
  • 8 oz spaghetti noodles
  • Grated parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Fresh basil and leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add the ground beef. Cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add the chopped onion and garlic to the skillet and cook for another 2-3 minutes until softened.
  3. Stir in the celery and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, beef broth, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Reduce heat to low and let it simmer for 30-45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  7. Drain spaghetti and serve over the bolognese sauce. Garnish with grated parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves if desired.
